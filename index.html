About Me

Who I Am

Hi, I'm Akshat Pandey — a student, developer, creator, and aspiring entrepreneur from Patna, India. My journey into technology began at a young age through blogging and website development. What started as simple curiosity gradually evolved into a passion for building things, solving problems, and turning ideas into reality.

My interests span technology, entrepreneurship, content creation, web development, electronics, finance, and Indian history and culture. I enjoy exploring new ideas and building projects that combine creativity with practical use.

My app development journey started with no-code platforms like Kodular and Niotron, then expanded into cloud technologies, Firebase, and IoT systems. I've worked with ESP8266, sensors, Blynk, and automation systems — bridging software with the physical world.

I am deeply inspired by India's rich heritage and believe that age should never limit ambition. Whether it's coding, creating content, or exploring markets, I always strive to become better than I was yesterday.

"To keep learning, keep building, and create things that leave a meaningful impact. Because every great journey starts with curiosity — and I'm just getting started."

My Journey

The Story So Far

From blogging in Bihar to shipping apps on the Play Store — here's how it unfolded.

2019 – 2021
The Beginning — Blogs & Websites
Growing up in Bihar, I was fascinated by how websites and digital products worked. I started blogging and exploring SEO, learning how the internet works — building on WordPress and Blogger, experimenting with Google tools and digital content. Curiosity turned into passion.
2021 – 2022
First Apps & IoT — The Kodular Era
I stepped into app development through no-code platforms like Kodular and Niotron. I learned how APIs work and started connecting apps to IoT devices — working with ESP8266, sensors, Blynk, Firebase, and automation systems. Software met hardware. I built dashboards, connected devices, and experimented with online business models.
2022 – 2023
The Pause — Stepping Back
Life pulled me toward academics and I stepped back from active app development. But the ideas never stopped. I kept exploring — AI tools, animated content creation, NFC products, affiliate marketing, social media monetization, finance, and trading. Every experiment taught something new.
2023 – 2025
Expanding — Content, Finance & Entrepreneurship
Content creation became a core part of my journey. I explored YouTube animation, history and finance storytelling, and educational content. I studied trading, cryptocurrency, and financial markets. I started experimenting with Amazon FBM/FBA, dropshipping, and digital entrepreneurship — building the mindset of someone who ships products, not just writes code.
2026 — Now ✦
The Return — Steak Timer on Google Play 🥩
After years away from native app development, I came back — this time with a real product. Steak Timer is my first professional app on the Google Play Store: a precision cooking timer with smart algorithms, beautiful animations, multi-steak coordination, cook history, and a $3.49/year Pro tier. Four years of ideas, experiments, and learning — shipped into one app. This is just the beginning.
