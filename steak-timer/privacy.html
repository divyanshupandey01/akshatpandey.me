01 Who We Are

Steak Timer ("the App") is developed and maintained by Akshat Pandey, an independent developer. This Privacy Policy applies to the Steak Timer mobile application available on Google Play.

If you have any questions about this policy, you can reach us at ashbuildsinpublic@gmail.com.

02 What We Collect

We collect the minimum data necessary to run the app and serve ads. Here is a full breakdown:

Data Type What It Is Why We Collect It
Device identifiers Advertising ID (GAID), device model, OS version Ad personalisation (Google AdMob)
Cook preferences Your saved defaults (cut type, thickness, method) Stored locally on your device only — never sent to our servers
Cook history Past cook entries and notes you create Stored locally on your device only — never sent to our servers

🔒 Your cook history and personal preferences are stored only on your device. We never upload them to any server.

04 Advertising

Free-tier users see a banner advertisement at the bottom of the main screen, served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device's Advertising ID to show personalised ads based on your interests.

You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalisation"

05 Children's Privacy

Steak Timer is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, please contact us at ashbuildsinpublic@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

07 Data Retention

Steak Timer does not operate any backend servers. Your cook history and saved preferences remain stored locally on your device until you clear app data or uninstall the app. Google AdMob and Google Play Services may collect and retain advertising or diagnostic data according to Google's privacy policy.
